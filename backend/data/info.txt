## Early Life

Shortly after his birth, his family moved to Udumalpet, a town located in the Tiruppur district of Tamil Nadu. Much of his childhood and formative years were spent there.

From a very young age, Suryaprakash was known for his curiosity and imagination. He often dreamed about achieving great things in life and was fascinated by technology, innovation, and the idea of building something meaningful that could impact others.
